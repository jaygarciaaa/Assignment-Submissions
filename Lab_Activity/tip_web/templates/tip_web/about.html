About the Technological Institute of the Philippines

Our Mission

The Technological Institute of the Philippines (TIP) is a non-sectarian, private institution founded in 1962. We are committed to fostering technological innovation and engineering excellence.

With campuses in Quezon City and Manila, TIP offers programs accredited by CHED and international bodies like ABET, emphasizing real-world skills and future-ready tech.
